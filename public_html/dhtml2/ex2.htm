Place-anywhere Hidden Menus

The menu below consists of a set of nested divs that can be placed anywhere on the page. A hidden menu will appear when you move your mouse on top of any menu name. This technique works in the latest versions of Internet Explorer, Netscape Navigator, and all browsers that support the W3C standard DOM and CSS.
